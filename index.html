FAQ – Translation Services

What types of files can you translate?

We can handle a wide variety of file formats, including Microsoft Office documents (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), PDFs, InDesign files, XML, HTML, and more. Just send us your files, and we'll take care of the rest.

How long does translation take?

The turnaround time depends on the word count, complexity, and language pair. A standard translation of up to 2,000 words can typically be delivered within 1-2 business days. We also offer express services for urgent projects.

Do you offer certified translations?

Yes, we provide certified translations for official documents such as birth certificates, legal contracts, and academic transcripts. These translations come with a certificate of accuracy for official use.

What languages do you support?

We support over 100 languages, covering major global and regional languages. Our network of native linguists ensures we can meet the needs of a diverse client base across various industries.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing is typically calculated on a per-word basis. The rate can vary depending on the language combination, subject matter complexity, and required turnaround time. We provide a detailed, transparent quote for every project.
